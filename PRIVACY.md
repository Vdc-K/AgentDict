# AgentDict Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-03-19**

## Overview

AgentDict is a browser extension that helps users learn AI and Agent technical terminology while browsing. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

AgentDict does **NOT** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage` API:

- **Learning progress**: Which terms you've viewed, their mastery status (new/faded/mastered), and view counts
- **User preferences**: Extension settings such as annotation density and display mode

## Data Usage

- Your learning data is used solely to personalize the learning experience (e.g., fading already-known terms)
- No data is shared with any third parties
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is collected
- No cookies are used

## Permissions

AgentDict requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your learning progress and preferences locally |
| `activeTab` | Detect terms on the current page you're viewing |
| Host permissions | Run on supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) |

## Network Requests

AgentDict makes **zero** network requests. It operates entirely offline after installation. All 193 terms and their definitions are bundled within the extension.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the extension. All locally stored data will be removed automatically.

You can also reset your learning progress from the extension popup without uninstalling.

## Children's Privacy

AgentDict does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/user/AgentDict).
